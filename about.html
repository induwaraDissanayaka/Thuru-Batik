About Batik Lanka

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Batik Lanka began as a small family workshop in the heart of Kandy. Our founder, Mrs. Sumanawathi, learned the ancient art of batik from her grandmother and dreamed of sharing this beautiful tradition with the world.

Today, we work with over 50 skilled artisans across Sri Lanka, preserving traditional techniques while creating contemporary designs that appeal to modern tastes.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote the ancient Sri Lankan art of batik, empower rural artisan communities, and bring authentic, handcrafted beauty to homes around the world.
